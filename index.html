❓ How to explore

👆Drag with your finger or mouse to look around space.
🤏Pinch with two fingers (or scroll the mouse wheel) to zoom in and out.
🪐Tap any planet (or its name) to fly to it in your rocket!
🔊Tap Read to me and your ship's computer reads the facts out loud.
🎯Follow the missions at the top to become a Space Captain!
🛂Every world you visit gives you a passport stamp. Collect all 11!
🌌Tap All at the bottom to zoom back out and see the whole solar system.
🤓Science note: real space is MUCH emptier — we squished the distances so you can see all the planets at once!
Planet texture maps: James Hastings-Trew / planetpixelemporium.com via the threex.planets project. Built with three.js. Sounds are made live by your computer — no downloads!
